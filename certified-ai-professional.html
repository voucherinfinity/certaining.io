Certified AI Professional

The Certainly Certified Artificial Intelligence Professional test is a globally recognized, vendor-neutral certification that certifies the knowledge and abilities required to work in the AI industry. This certification is suitable for students pursuing computer science or engineering degrees who want to build a strong foundation in AI and related subfields, as well as professionals working in data science, machine learning, or AI who want to broaden their knowledge and skills. The certification includes six domains: Introduction to AI, Math Fundamentals for AI, Machine Learning, Deep Learning AI in Practice, and Six Programming for AI.



Skills You Will Learn

  • AI: applications, kinds, subfields, history, and ethical issues.
  • span Fundamental mathematical and statistical ideas are required for developing and training AI models.
  • How to use machine learning algorithms and techniques including supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning to address real-world issues.
  • Learn about neural network construction, the backpropagation method, and its variations, as well as advanced deep learning subjects including GANs, transfer learning, and reinforcement learning with deep neural networks.
  • Understanding real-world AI applications such as natural language processing, computer vision, data preparation, feature engineering, model deployment, and monitoring facilitates the application of AI technology to challenging challenges.
  • Proficiency in data processing and visualization using Python libraries like Pandas and Matplotlib, as well as an understanding of R programming principles and machine learning methodologies including decision trees and clustering methods.


Exam Domains of Certaining Certified AI Professional

  • Module 1: Introduction to AI - 10%

  • Module 2: Math Fundamentals for AI - 20%

  • Module 3: Machine Learning - 30%

  • Module 4: Deep Learning - 25%

  • Module 5: AI in Practice - 10% Module

  • Module 6: Programming for AI - 5%


Format of Exam

  • Multiple choice questions

  • No. of questions: 80

  • Max marks: 80, Required marks: 56 (i.e.70%).

  • Duration: 120 minutes

  • Closed book
