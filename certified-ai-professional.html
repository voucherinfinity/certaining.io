Certified AI Professional

The Certaining Certified Artificial Intelligence Professional certification exam is a global vendor-neutral certification that validates the knowledge and skills required to work in the Artificial Intelligence domain. This certification is ideal for students pursuing computer science or engineering degrees who want to gain a solid foundation in AI and its subfields as well as professionals working in the field of data science, machine learning, or AI to expand their knowledge and skills. The certification covers 6 domains including Introduction to AI, Math Fundamentals for AI, Machine Learning, Deep Learning AI in Practice,6 Programming for AI.



Skills You Will Learn

  • AI, its applications, types, subfields, history, and associated ethical considerations.
  • span Fundamental mathematical and statistical concepts that are essential for building and training AI models.
  • How to apply machine learning algorithms and techniques, including supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning, to solve real-world problems.
  • Understand neural network architecture, backpropagation algorithm, and its variants, as well as advanced deep learning topics such as GANs, transfer learning, and reinforcement learning with deep neural networks.
  • Understanding practical applications of AI, including natural language processing, computer vision, data preparation, feature engineering, and model deployment and monitoring, enabling the application of AI techniques to solve complex problems.
  • Proficiency in data manipulation and visualization using Python libraries like Pandas and Matplotlib, as well as an introduction to R programming basics for data analysis and machine learning techniques such as decision trees and clustering algorithms.


Exam Domains of Certaining Certified AI Professional

  • Module 1: Introduction to AI - 10%

  • Module 2: Math Fundamentals for AI - 20%

  • Module 3: Machine Learning - 30%

  • Module 4: Deep Learning - 25%

  • Module 5: AI in Practice - 10% Module

  • Module 6: Programming for AI - 5%


Format of Exam

  • Multiple choice questions

  • No. of questions: 80

  • Max marks: 80, Required marks: 56 (i.e.70%).

  • Duration: 120 minutes

  • Closed book
