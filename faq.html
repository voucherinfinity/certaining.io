Exam vouchers are prepaid codes that cover part or all of the cost of certification exams, making them a cost-effective option for test-takers.

An early expiration voucher typically refers to a prepaid voucher or coupon that has a limited validity period, which expires earlier than standard vouchers. This means that the voucher must be used within a specified timeframe, usually shorter than usual, to redeem its value. If the voucher is not used before the expiration date, it becomes invalid, and the holder cannot redeem it for its intended purpose. Early expiration vouchers are often used for promotional purposes or to encourage prompt action from customers.
