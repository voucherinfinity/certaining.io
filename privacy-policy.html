Beingcert LLC is one of the leading Int’l certification bodies headquartered in Virginia. Being a hallmark of quality certification, we at Beingcert respects your concerns for privacy. Keeping your utmost trust is our priority. In this Privacy Policy, we will be detailing out the policies of information collection and information collected and maintained from our end.

Our website is being handled with an experienced team of experts that ensures to keep the information accurate and updated time-to-time. Our objective is to provide users with up-to-date, authentic, and unique information. Hence, without giving any prior notification, we may change the information as or when required.

We value our customers, audience, and viewers. It is our responsibility to upkeep their trust and confidence. Any information provided by our prestigious clientele and individuals is not sold or traded with any third party without your valuable consent.

Beingcert does not indulge in any activity that would hamper the privacy of our valuable clientele. The information we collect is solely taken at the discretion of our clients. We do not depend on any party to share the data of our clients.

For a successful booking of the examination, we may take your personal contact information such as name, age, email address, telephone no, postal address to ensure the candidate duly proceeds with the examination. Once you provide us with the accurate email credentials, we would assign you unique credentials, respectively.

Your information is crucial in helping us to serve you better. We would need your details to administer, assess, develop examinations. Please ensure to key in the name in English without making use of any special characters. Since our website is characters’ sensitive, special characters are not recognized by the system and shall be marked as an invalid entry.

If you fail to provide us with the accurate information, then the certification department will not be able to process the certificate. Invalid email address is not easily accessible, processing of such certifications!

We also need your details to administer, assess, and develop examinations. We will not be held responsible if any details shared with us are false or inaccurate in any aspect.

Beingcert uses Cookies as per the law to enhance your online experience and track website performance. If you disable the cookies, all the services and the features on the website may not function correctly, and you might fail to use some of the functionality available on the site.

For any assistance or queries, or if they need to make modifications to the information that we are holding, then our clientele or audience can directly write to us at info@certaining.com
