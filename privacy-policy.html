  • Privacy Policy

Certaining LLC is a respected global certification authority headquartered in Virginia. Certaining, as a mark of quality certification, respects your privacy concerns. Maintaining your trust is our top priority. This Privacy Policy describes our information-collecting policies, as well as the data we collect and maintain.

Our website is managed by an experienced team of specialists who ensure that the content is correct and updated regularly. Our objective is to give users with timely, accurate, and unique information. As a result, we reserve the right to alter the information as needed and without previous warning.

We value our consumers, audience, and viewers. We should keep their trust and confidence. Any information provided by our valued clients and individuals will never be sold or shared with a third party unless you expressly consent.

Certaining does not take any actions that would risk the privacy of our valued clients. Our clients decide which information we collect. We do not rely on third parties to provide our clients' information.

We may gather your personal contact information, such as your name, age, email address, phone number, and postal address, to ensure that the applicant takes the exam. We will assign you unique credentials once we have received the right email credentials.

Your information is crucial to helping us better serve you. We need your information to conduct, assess, and create exams. Please put your name in English, without any unusual characters. Because our website is character-sensitive, the system does not detect unusual characters and reports them as invalid entries.

If you do not supply us with accurate information, the certification department will be unable to process your certificate. Invalid email addresses make it difficult to process these certifications!

We also require your information to conduct, evaluate, and generate exams. We will not be held liable if the information submitted to us is fraudulent or incorrect in any way.

Certaining uses cookies under applicable legislation to improve your online experience and track website performance. If you disable cookies, not all of the website's services and features will work properly, and you may be unable to use some of its capabilities.
